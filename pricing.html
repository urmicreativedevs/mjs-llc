Why Choose Marijuana Software Solutions

Compliance

Marijuana Software Cannabis POS helps cannabis retailers stay compliant with state and local regulations. It includes built-in compliance features such as age verification, ID scanning, and purchase limits.

Easy to use

The user interface of Marijuana Software Cannabis POS is intuitive and easy to use, allowing budtenders to quickly and accurately process transactions. It includes features such as product search, barcode scanning, and customized order modifiers to streamline the sales process.

Inventory management

Marijuana Software Cannabis POS includes powerful inventory management features that allow retailers to track their stock levels, receive real-time inventory alerts, and manage transfers between locations. It also includes tools for managing vendor relationships and purchase orders.

Reporting

The system includes a range of reporting options that allow retailers to analyze sales data, track employee performance, and monitor inventory levels. These reports can be customized to meet the needs of individual retailers.

Integration

Marijuana Software Cannabis POS can be integrated with a range of third-party applications and services, including Metrc, QuickBooks, and Leafly, to further streamline the retail process. It also includes a built-in loyalty program for rewarding repeat customers.
