Private Cloud

For enterprise customers, our Private Cloud service offers exclusivity and enhanced features. Enjoy a private copy of our Marijuana Software service tailored specifically for your use. For only $300 per month for the first location and $150/month per additional location, you'll have always fast service, a private database, and a custom branded portal. Please note, an annual contract is required for this plan.

Backup Server

Never compromise on business continuity with our Backup Server solution. Ideal for businesses that can't afford any downtime, this on-premises disaster recovery option ensures your operations stay running smoothly. Our HP-Elite Desk mini PC runs Marijuana Software locally at your location, guaranteeing fast service and resilience. Connect it to your local WiFi router and battery backup unit, and it will continue to function even during internet or power outages. The Backup Server offers secure access within your shop, ensuring data protection. Pair it with the Private Cloud plan for a comprehensive connected in-premises/off-premises solution.
