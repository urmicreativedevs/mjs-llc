Marijuana Software's delivery application is an essential tool for dispensaries that want to offer their customers a convenient and hassle-free delivery service while ensuring compliance with state regulations. Our application allows you to post delivery data directly to METRC, the state-mandated cannabis tracking system, making it easy to manage your inventory and stay compliant. With our delivery application, you can manage delivery schedules, track driver locations, and receive real-time updates on deliveries. Features

Inventory and Distribution Management Platforms

Our wholesale software includes a powerful inventory management system that helps you manage your stock levels across multiple locations. You can easily track product movements, manage stock levels, and monitor inventory levels in real-time. We also offer distribution management tools that help you manage deliveries, track driver location, and provide real-time updates to customers.

Packaging and Labeling System

Our wholesale software includes a packaging and labeling system that makes it easy for you to create custom product labels with compliance information. You can print product labels with custom branding, barcodes, and other relevant information.

Simplified Cost-Per-Gram Calculation

Our software includes a cost-per-gram calculator that helps you determine the cost of each gram of product you sell. This makes it easy for you to determine your profit margins and adjust prices accordingly.

METRC Compliance

Our software is fully compliant with METRC regulations, making it easy for you to stay compliant with state regulations. You can easily track sales, manage customer data, and generate reports to ensure compliance.

Sales Management Software

Our wholesale software includes a powerful sales management system that helps you manage your sales data, monitor performance, and identify trends. You can track sales data, monitor inventory levels, and identify trends to make informed business decisions.
